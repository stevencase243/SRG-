🏁 SRG On-Track Conduct

These guidelines promote clean, respectful racing while preserving the competitive and fun nature of league racing. Not every incident will result in a penalty, but all drivers are expected to race with awareness and consideration for others.

1 · General Conduct

1.1Race with respect. This is for fun, not win-at-all-costs.
1.2Avoid overly aggressive moves that rely on another driver taking evasive action to avoid contact.
1.3If a move forces a car that is alongside off track, the position should be given back or relinquished, depending on who gained the advantage.

2 · Passing

Establishing Position
2.1Avoid contact. Both drivers share responsibility.
2.2Overlap (alongside) exists when a car's front nose is ahead of the front edge of the defending car's rear tire.
2.3If overlap exists, both drivers should leave racing room. Drivers should allow additional margin where possible to account for netcode and latency.
2.4No overlap by turn-in = the trailing car should yield.
Passing Driver Responsibilities
2.5The passing driver is responsible for attempting a safe pass.
2.6A passing attempt should not rely on the other driver taking evasive action to avoid contact.
2.7Do not leave the racing surface to complete a pass.
Defending Driver Responsibilities
2.8Drivers may choose a defensive line before the braking zone but should not make reactive moves in response to a passing attempt.
2.9Drivers may not move under braking and should maintain a predictable line into corner entry.
2.10Blue flags do not require an immediate yield, but drivers should not make a passing attempt unnecessarily difficult.

3 · Rejoining

3.1Use a spotter or blind-spot tool when rejoining or exiting pits.
3.2Rejoin parallel to the track at a safe speed.
3.3Do not interfere with cars on track. Be predictable until up to pace.
3.4If you cause an incident while rejoining, it is your responsibility.
3.5Do not cross the racing line unsafely. Reverse if needed.
3.6If off track, yield to all cars before rejoining.
3.7If you lose control, hold the brakes and avoid unpredictable movement.
3.8Only rejoin once the car is under control.
3.9Serve slow-down penalties off the racing line. No blocking or defending.

4 · Track Limits

4.1Follow iRacing track limits.
